What is Flux?

Flux is a one month gathering of creative and open minded individuals in vibrant locations around the world. We like to cultivate a space for technology, art, peer to peer education, and a location independent lifestyle. We believe the time spent working and living together will facilitate connections, friendship, and collaboration.

Who is Flux for?

  • Digital Nomads who are looking to connect with the local and global community
  • People who are interested in a location independent life style but don't know where to start
  • Artists, hackers and technology enthusiast of any kind
